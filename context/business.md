# Tuned For — Business Context

## About

**Company:** Tuned For LLC
**Brand:** tunedfor.ai
**Owner:** Kevin
**Model:** Sandbox / passive play AI projects portfolio. Not a startup with obligations.

## Philosophy

- Build when there's time. Let it grow organically.
- Genuinely useful, not a startup pitch
- Nothing feels like a product launch — it just works
- Zero jargon. Speaks to real people, not developers.

## Architecture

- **tunedfor.ai** — home base, prompt library, public face
- **[product].tunedfor.ai** — sub-projects spin up under the same domain
- Each product is an independent experiment, not a roadmap commitment

## Constraints

- Solo operator — build for maintainability, not team scale
- Passive revenue model preferred — BYOK, pay-per-feature, not subscription ops
- No ads. Ever.
- Free tier must be real and functional — not a bait-and-switch

## Monetization Defaults (unless product.md overrides)

- Books 1–3 / first N uses: free on Tuned For API keys
- Beyond free tier: BYOK (Bring Your Own Key)
- Keys stored client-side only — never touch Tuned For servers
- Future upsells: print-quality PDF, saved library, physical fulfillment

## Brand Voice

- Warm, confident, zero jargon
- The tone of a smart friend who happens to know a lot about the domain
- Nothing feels like a startup pitch — it just works

## Legal

- LLC formation pending CPA consult
- Passive loss / hobby-loss rules, W-2 offset, Section 179 hardware are key questions
- COPPA: avoid PII collection on free tier to sidestep compliance burden
